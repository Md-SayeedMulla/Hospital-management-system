BLOOD BANK

Introduction

The aim of the blood bank is to give safe blood to the needy patients.

It starts with selection of blood donors, screening of the donors is done thoroughly

* Blood grouping is done by automatic machines that is Gel technology.

* Screening of the blood sample is done by cheminuminissions method The tests done are HIV(1&2),HBS Ag,HCV,VDRL & Maleria Parasites,VDRL as per FDI guidelines.

* Antibody screening 3 cell panel is done here regularly if required go for higher cell panel (11 cell Panel) & ABO anti body screening also done here. Granulocytes separation & stem cell theorapy will be started shortly.

* For Thellessemia 42 patients has under gone Bone Marrow Transplantation from this centre.
